modulo calculator

%


Answer

When doing a modulo calculation, the remainder of the division is returned. For example, 5 % 2 = 1, because 2 goes into 5 once, with a remainder of 1. The modulo operator is used to find the remainder of a division problem. It is also known as the modulus operator.
